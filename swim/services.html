Our Services

We offer engaging and meaningful swim instruction to children aged 2 and up.

Our lessons have a 30 minute runtime per individual swimmer.

As for beginner swimmers, regardless of age, water safety techniques are the first thing we focus on (underwaters, floating on back, etc).

For intermediate swimmers, we tend to focus on a variety of essential swimming techniques, building up to to the introduction of stroke swimming (flutter kick on back/stomach, arm strokes, water rolls, etc).

We also have much to offer advanced/rec swimmers (specific stroke improvement, dive/turn work, etc)!
