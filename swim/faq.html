FAQs

Answering your FAQs regarding our swim lessons!

What should I bring for my child's lesson?

Please bring a pair of goggles, and a kickboard if you have it! Also, don't forget sunscreen!

How should I prepare my pool for the lesson?

If possible, please have your pool heated! This will help your child to be as comfortable as possible throughout the lesson. Of course, if you're meeting me at your neighborhood's local pool, you don't have to worry about this! I'll find a quiet area within the public pool to conduct the lesson.

Should I be there for the lesson to watch over everything?

I'd say in most cases, the children I teach perform better when their parents are out of view, so this is what I recommend. However, if you'd like to watch your child throughout the lesson, I'd suggest that you find an area a bit out of the way to watch over your child!
