Hailey

Hello there! My name is Hailey Usher and I am a sophomore at the University of Colorado, Boulder, where I am studying Engineering and Engineering Management. I am also a member of CU's club swim team, and I have previous experience as a swimmer for both SRV and many years on the Crow Canyon Sea Lions.

My years of being coached have taught me the importance of good coaching, which led me to become a junior coach for the Sea Lions. Through this role, I learned the value of clear communication, patience, creating a fun team environment, and motivating children at various skill levels. As someone who has been a swimmer for most of my life, I am passionate about sharing my love for the sport and helping others learn while having fun.

I have been successful in introducing new swimmers to the water, and I am eager to work with your child this summer. Thank you for considering me as a coach!
